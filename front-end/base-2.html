Introduction

Welcome to my little corner of the internet! This site is home to my latest project, Matcher AI, along with a bit about me, my work, and how to get in touch.

So, what’s Matcher AI all about? It’s a simple tool I whipped up to see how well job descriptions match my skills. You paste a job description, and it gives you a percentage score on how likely I am to be a good fit. It’s no-nonsense and honest—if the role says “must bake cakes,” and I’m not a baker (even though I make a mean banana bread), Matcher AI will let you know.

While you’re here, feel free to wander around. Check out my CV page for more on my experience and even download a copy if you’re curious. And if you’ve got thoughts, questions, or feedback—especially about Matcher AI—head over to the Contact page and drop me a line.

Thanks for stopping by, and I hope you enjoy the site!
